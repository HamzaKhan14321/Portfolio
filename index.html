01 About Me

I’m Hamza Khan — a full-time tech content creator from Nowshera, KPK, Pakistan with over 7 years of experience producing in-depth tech reviews and analysis content.

My content covers smartphones, laptops, gaming PCs, chipsets, and cameras across YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and X. I’ve built a reputation for honest, thorough reviews that serve real buyers — not just tech enthusiasts.

Every review I produce follows a strict 13-part methodology covering Design, Display, Sound, Performance, Gaming, OS, Battery, Camera, Connectivity, Security, Price, and Final Verdict — ensuring my audience always gets the complete picture.

I handle the full production pipeline: scripting, filming with DSLRs and smartphones, editing in Filmora 14/15 and CapCut Pro, thumbnails, SEO, and platform publishing.
